HOW TO MAKE
TEA

At the very least, tea is a flavourful way of getting enough
fluid into your body each day. On top of that, studies have
shown teas can help protect your teeth and your heart

PREPERATION‚úîÔ∏è

  1. Run the tap a little so the water's nicely aerated, and
    only boil it once to keep the oxygen level up. Oxygen
    in water helps flavour!
  2. Pop a tea bag into your mug (always a big mug)
  3. Pour the hot water over the tea bag and stir briefly.
  4. Tea needs time to unlock all its flavour, so give it 3-4
    minutes to do its thing. This is a perfect time to grab a
    sneaky cookie or daydream about vacations.
  5. Before removing the tea bag, gently squish it with a
    spoon against the side of the mug. Not too much or
    you'll make it bitter.
  6. If you want, throw in some milk or sugar or
    honey or nothing else at all.
  7. Enjoy!
